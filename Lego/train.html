  • Signals and the like
    • Frank Buiting's details on how to convert the 12V signals for use with 9V trains
  • Automatic/remote uncouplers are one of the most desired things in model railroading. When you are simulating reality, it is undesireable to introduce the HOG (Hand of God). Unfortuanately LEGO trains provide special challenges for uncoupling because the couplers are magnetic, which requires prying the cars apart to separate the couplers.
    • Rick's Lego Train Whistle Stop (remote decoupler)
    • Leo Dorst's LEGO Designs
    • LUDO (SOETE)'S LEGO® Models
  • Automatic/remote control of turnouts (points or switches) is another desireable thing. Again, LEGO turnouts introduce special problems due to the force required to move the points from one position to the other.
