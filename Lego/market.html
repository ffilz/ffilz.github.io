JAMBALAYA

Jambalaya is a concept where someone puts a bunch of LEGO® bits and pieces they no longer want into a box, and then calls for people interested in trading with the box. The box is then sent on a merry tour with people trading a few pieces with what's in the box.

Tommy Johnson keeps a list of where several have been and who's up next here..
