Frank's LEGO® Page

Welcome to my fan page dedicated to LEGO® brand construction toys. You will find pictures of models I have created (commonly known as MOCs or my own creations), and links to many reference resources about The Brick (as the basic element of LEGO® brand construction toys is often called by adoring fans - who themselves are often known as AFOLs or Adult Fans of LEGO). You will also find hundreds of links to other fan's pages and creations.
